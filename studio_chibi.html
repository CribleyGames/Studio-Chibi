Welcome to Studio Chibi

Studio Chibi is a group of some of the largest content creators in the Sky: Children of the Light Community. Set up by British Sky Youtuber XK Generations, he has managed to get in contact with some of the largest content creators in the community. Some of the orginisations most known projects have been the Sky Youtube community rewind which has been given attention by Thatgamecompany, the makers of Sky, Journey and Flower.

Logo Banner Concepts (Designed by XK)

What is Studio Chibi?

Studio Chibi is a small YouTuber Hangout of currently 15 people run by XK and Moderated by Harbin. The group was founded by the making of the Sky Rewind project and XK is organising to run more projects in the group. Some members of the group include ThySkyGuy, Emptea, Crafting Kitten, Toyz, Irinrin and many more!


The aim of Studio Chibi is to bring all parts of life from the sky community together and celebrate the culture of the community of Sky and Thatgamecompany. This is why most of the members of Studio chibi has been from many backgrounds of the sky community. Including Meme Creators (Such as Crafting Kitten and Emptea), Tutorial experts (Such as BluSky and ThySkyGuy) and Sky update experts (such as Toyz and JaneSky).

All of these content creators have been found from all across the world. From America to Indonesia and many more. Studio Chibi is a worldwide known organisation with its aim to inspire content creators from around the world.

The company is run through the XK Generations Channel. Linked below on this website

Who is XK Generations?

XK Generations is a Content creator based in the UK who is most well known for Sky videos, Some of his most well known videos include Cosmetics Stereotypes, Sky Rewind 2020 and multiple meme and tutorial videos. XK has also worked with some of the largest sky YouTubers in the community, including Fox Band (aka Thatskyfox), Toyz Gaming, Emptea and BluSky naming just a few content creators XK has worked with on Youtube projects. XK also makes non sky related content as well including Minecraft videos with friends and Nintendo related videos.

Studio Chibi News

OUR PROJECTS

Rewind 2020

This was the first project run by Studio Chibi and was a large project that was worked on by XK and many other content creators from the Sky Youtuber community. The development took 1 month to prepare and organise along with 3 weeks of editing and a week of finalising to make this video. On release on Boxing Day 2020, it went viral all over the community and now sits on 4000 views and XK's 6th most popular video on his channel along with 500+ likes from the community.

15 Years of Thatgamecompany

The latest Studio Chibi production to celebrate the amazing achievements of Thatgamecompany. The video premieres at 4pm UK time May 15th 2021. The video was directed and made by XK Generations, along with editing by XK and BluSky, Voiceovers from XK, Aztcc and Cribley and Featured cameos taken from previous recordings used for edits.
