Studio Chibi

What is Studio Chibi?

Studio Chibi is a group of some of the largest content creators in the Sky: Children of the Light Community. Set up by British Sky Youtuber XK Generations, he has managed to get in contact with some of the largest content creators in the community. Some of the orginisations most known projects have been the Sky Youtube community rewind which has been given attention by ThatGameCompany, the makers of Sky, Journey and Flower.

The aim of Studio Chibi is to bring all parts of life from the Sky community together and celebrate the culture of the community of Sky and ThatGameCompany. This is why most of the members of Studio Chibi has been from many backgrounds of the Sky community. Including meme creators (Crafting Kitten and Emptea), tutorial experts (BluSky and ThySkyGuy), speedrunners (Aztcc and Cribley), and Sky update experts (Toyz and JaneSky).

All of these content creators have been found from all across the world. From America to Indonesia and many more. Studio Chibi is a worldwide known organisation with its aim to inspire content creators and players from around the world.

Who is XK Generations?

XK Generations is a Content creator based in the UK who is most well known for Sky videos, Some of his most well known videos include Cosmetics Stereotypes, Sky Rewind 2020 and multiple meme and tutorial videos. XK has also worked with some of the largest sky YouTubers in the community, including Fox Band (aka Thatskyfox), Toyz Gaming, Emptea and BluSky naming just a few content creators XK has worked with on Youtube projects. XK also makes non sky related content as well including Minecraft videos with friends and Nintendo related videos.

How do I join the Studio Chibi Team?

We are currently open for Applications!

We are currently open for applications for certain roles for people to volenteer into the Studio Chibi!

Content creators are at the heart of the project, they are the heart and soul which runs the entire Studio Chibi. To become part of the members at Studio Chibi, you can either be invited in by XK or you can apply here. If you are needing to apply, the requirements are:

• Is involved in making Sky content specifically for the Sky community.

• They don't have to be just a sky channel, they could also be someone who makes other games.

• Must have over a certain following of subscribers. If youtube, Applicants must have over 250 subscribers on youtube. This is so that the community knows who you are.

• Must have at least one video with over 7,500 views. Or a total channel viewing of over 50,000 views.

We are now able to hire a moderator for Studio Chibi! This is to make sure questions can be answered about projects and details. The requirements are:

• Must be involved in the Sky community.

• Moderates a Sky YouTube community.

• Has Creative ideas to help out with planning of projects.

We are currently looking for artists to design original art for Studio Chibi to be added onto thumbnails. The requirements are:

•You must show some of your Original artworks.

•Preferably it would be nice to show some Sky art as well.

• Has Creative ideas about what to draw.

Thumbnails are an important part of Studio Chibi and the first impressions of a video is always the thumbnails. Since XK isn't very good at making thumbnails he is hiring a content creator to show new people who studio chibi are. The requirements are:

• Uses Photoshop or a Photo Editing software of some sorts.

• Shows a piece of photoshopped work.

• Can convert images and artworks into PNG's easily.

• If you are considering of being a thumbnail artist, you may be asked to also make promotional posters to be shared throughout the community as well.
